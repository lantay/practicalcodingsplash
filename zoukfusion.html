A Style of

Solo Dance

Zouk Fusion parties are the ideal dance parties. Beautiful movements and structure from Zouk allow people to feel powerful in their bodies while freedom from Fusion lets people express themselves and makes Zouk Fusion the easiest dance style to learn. It can be done on any floor and is perfect for people who might frequent yoga centers, bars, and dance studios alike.

A Style of

Partner Dance

Partner dance is the easiest way for people to meet each other--platonically and intimately. People dancing together raises the energy of the party further than many people have ever seen, and does so almost effortlessly. Zouk Fusion in particular feels extremely safe yet very deep at the same time, and the way it is taught makes sure that people understand boundaries and feel comfortable


Meet Mason


With 17 years of experience in embodiment practice ranging from Martial Arts to Music, Yoga, Meditation, and Dance, Mason created Zouk Fusion as a way to celebrate the beautiful body movements and masculine-feminine polar energy of Zouk while allowing for the freedom of expression of Fusion with the focus on mindfulness from Meditation and Yoga.

Mason has taught Zouk Fusion workshops for the past two years in North Carolina, California, and Bali. Mason's workshops typically consist of an hour lesson where students learn dance techniques as well as how to feel the flow of energy and space holding in solo and partner dance, followed by 1-2 hours of free dance where people can let go and dance with each other and themselves.
